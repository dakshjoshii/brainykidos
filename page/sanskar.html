Sanskar classes

BrainyKidos | Fun way to learn

Sanskar Varg program focuses on the harmonious overall development of children.

By way of teaching children to chant Sanskrit mantras, mantras will help children overcome speech impediments but not just that; the breathing patterns and the vibrations that are produced by the children while chanting the mantras help them in building a positive sense of self.

Sanskar Varg is, hence, considered to be very effective in enhancing the mental wellness of growing children.
