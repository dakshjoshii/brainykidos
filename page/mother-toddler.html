BrainyKidos | Fun way to learn

Parent-child relationship is possibly the best in the world, and we feel privileged to strengthen it through our exclusive Parent Toddler Program. It is the best way to transit your child into a social environment like a preschool but with a parent assisting the child.

  • Parents get quality time with their child
  • Children are introduced to a social set up which aides in their socio-emotional development
  • Helps parents with tips and tricks to engage children meaningfully
  • Opportunity to observe and analyze the development of children
  • Prepares children to transit to the preschool environment
  • Opportunity for parents to bond with other like-minded parents


This program helps develop the cognitive, physical, language and social skills of children as they grow. This program helps to improve motor skills, kinesthetic skills, linguistic skills and cognitive skills. Not only do children learn from the Parent Toddler Program, but it is also a great way for parents to learn how to engage children meaningfully.

Toddler Program is specially designed to help toddlers in the age group of 3 months to 2 years. It is usually a series of sessions spread over weeks to make sure children feel confident.
